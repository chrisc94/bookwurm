Bookwurm adds digital conveniences to physical reading.

Find out how

Track your reading. Figure out what to read next.

By using Bookwurm's smart bookmark, tracking reading sessions is made easy. Simply snap the bookmark onto your wrist to begin tracking.

Once you remove the bookmark from your wrist, information about your reading session is automatically transferred to the companion app.

You can set goals related to your reading and get suggestions on what kinds of books to start reading next.
